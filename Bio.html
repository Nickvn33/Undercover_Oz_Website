Bio

Undercover Oz – Great Aussie Hits and More!

Established in 2015, we are Melbourne’s quintessential cover band, bringing high-energy live music to every performance. With a renewed lineup and a deep passion for Aussie anthems and classic pub favorites, we know how to get the crowd singing, dancing, and rocking all night long.

With a setlist of over 100 songs, we deliver an unmatched variety of hits, covering everything from classic rock to Aussie legends and crowd-pleasing favorites. Our signature three-part harmonies—with Jess, Toby, and Roland—create a rich, powerful sound that elevates every song.

As a six-piece powerhouse, our keyboard and saxophone add a unique and dynamic edge to our performances, giving us the versatility to cover just about anything. Our full production setup—including our own PA and lighting system—ensures we can deliver a professional, high-energy show on any stage, anywhere.

Unlike many cover bands, we are 100% live—no backing tracks, no shortcuts—just raw energy, killer musicianship, and the flexibility to read the crowd and keep the party going. Whether it’s a pub gig, corporate event, private function, or festival, we bring decades of experience and a love for live music, making every performance memorable and unique.

We’re not just a cover band—we’re keeping the spirit of great live music alive, the way it was meant to be before technology replaced real musicians with backing tracks. If you want a genuine, electrifying live music experience, book UndercoverOz for your next event and get ready for an unforgettable night of rock and roll that will have everyone on their feet, singing along, and partying hard.
